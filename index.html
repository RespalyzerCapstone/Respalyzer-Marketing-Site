Your Respiratory Health Companion
We aim to analyze your cough and breathing patterns to determine if you have any respiratory illnesses
We do this by recording your coughing and/or breathing patterns.
Then, we send it to a trained AI which should return your results immediately
If your results come back positive, no worries!
Detection is the first step, and we commend you for being so health conscious
Please note that here at Respalyzer, we do not aim to replace medical professionals
Rather, we hope this app will promote health conciousness
